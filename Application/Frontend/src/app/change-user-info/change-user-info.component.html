Account Deleteion:

Just before deleting your account, it's important for us to let you know the consequences of this action:

  • All files (folders and maps) that only you have administrator permission on will be permanently deleted from the system
    And so do all the other permissions and ability of other users in the system to access these files
    For this reason: We recommend that before deleting your user - give Owner permission for at least one other user
  • Your user account (password and username) will be permanently deleted from the system and will no longer be able to log in until you re-register
