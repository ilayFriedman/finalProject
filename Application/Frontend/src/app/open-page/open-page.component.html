ME-Mapper

Mean-Ends Maps (ME-Maps), which focuses on know-how-explicating of how to efficiently perform tasks and solve problems.
The graphical representation of the ME-maps allows for a concise view on the one hand,
while still maintaining the required details to analyze the knowledge on the other. Moreover, the new representation allows for new searching techniques In this web application
we allows a management of ME-maps by means of creation,
collaborative work, searching capabilities, dual representation, and resource management.
